Overview

What We Collect

Barway itself collects no personal data. All videos, lift records, and bar path data are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to any server.

The app displays ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect limited data to serve relevant ads. See the section below for details.

Advertising

Google AdMob

Barway uses Google AdMob to display banner ads. Google may collect the following data to serve ads:

Data Purpose
Device identifiers (IDFA) Personalized advertising (only if you grant permission)
IP address / approximate location Regional ad targeting
Device type, OS version, language Ad compatibility and targeting
Ad interaction data Measuring ad performance

Google's data collection is governed by the Google Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads in Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising on your device.

Permissions

Device Access

Barway requests the following permissions solely to provide app functionality. No data accessed through these permissions leaves your device.

Permission Why Required
Camera Record lift videos directly in the app Optional
Photo Library (read) Import existing training videos Yes
Photo Library (write) Save bar path overlay images to your camera roll Yes

iOS will prompt you for each permission the first time it is needed. You can review or revoke permissions at any time in Settings → Barway.

Your Data

Retention & Deletion

All lift records and videos are stored locally on your device. You can delete individual lifts from within the app at any time, or remove all data by uninstalling Barway.

We have no access to your data and cannot delete it on your behalf.

Children

Children's Privacy

Barway is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us so we can address it.
