Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Soundora is an ambient sound mixer for iOS. This policy explains what data is collected, by whom, and how it is used.

01

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our own servers.

All user preferences — saved presets and favorites — are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage APIs and are never sent anywhere.

02

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Soundora displays banner ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect data to serve and measure ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA, if you grant permission)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Ad interaction events (impressions, taps)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)

If you decline the App Tracking Transparency prompt, only non-personalized ads are shown. Google's data collection for non-personalized ads is limited to contextual and fraud-prevention signals.

03

App Tracking Transparency

On first launch, Soundora will ask for permission to track you across apps and websites using your device's Advertising Identifier (IDFA). This is used solely to show more relevant ads via AdMob.

Allow Personalized ads may be shown based on your interests.
Decline Only non-personalized ads are shown. App functionality is unaffected.

You can change this permission at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

04

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account or sign-in required
  • No name, email, or contact information
  • No location data (beyond what AdMob may infer from IP)
  • No microphone or camera access
  • No analytics or crash reporting services

05

Children's Privacy

Soundora is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information via the app, please contact us so we can address it.

06

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when the app's functionality or third-party services change. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
