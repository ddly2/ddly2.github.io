Privacy Policy

PReak  ·  Effective date: February 22, 2026

The short version

PReak stores your workout data only on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information. The only data that leaves your device is through Google AdMob, which serves ads and may use your device's advertising identifier if you grant permission through the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt.

1. Information We Collect

PReak is designed to work entirely offline. Your workout data — exercises, weights, reps, dates, and notes — is stored locally on your device using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any server.

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Your location
  • Your workout data
  • Your device contacts, camera, or microphone

2. Advertising — Google AdMob

PReak displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob (Google LLC). AdMob may collect and use certain information to serve ads, which may include:

  • Your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS)
  • IP address and approximate location derived from it
  • Device type, operating system version, and language
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policy. You can review Google's data practices at policies.google.com/privacy.

App Tracking Transparency (iOS 14.5+)
Before AdMob can access your device's advertising identifier, iOS will show you an App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt asking for your permission. If you deny permission, AdMob will still serve ads, but they will not be personalized using your IDFA. You can change this choice at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

To opt out of personalized advertising by Google across apps and websites, you can also visit adssettings.google.com.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

All workout data you enter is stored only on your device and is under your full control. You can delete all data at any time by using the Clear All Data option in the app's Settings tab, or by deleting the app from your device.

We do not maintain any servers or databases containing your personal information, so there is nothing for us to delete on our end.

4. Children's Privacy

PReak is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us and we will take steps to address the situation.

5. Your Rights — GDPR (EEA / UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Because we do not collect any personal data ourselves, these rights apply primarily to data held by Google AdMob. You may exercise your rights with Google directly at myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy.

6. Your Rights — CCPA (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected about you and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. PReak does not sell personal information. The personal information collected by Google AdMob is subject to Google's CCPA disclosures, which can be found in their privacy policy.

7. Third-Party Links and Services

The app does not contain links to third-party websites. The only third-party service integrated into the app is Google AdMob, described in Section 2.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
